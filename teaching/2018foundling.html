Course description

A condensed graduate-level overview of main concepts in Linguistics for students in the Cognitive Systems program who have no background in this.

  • morphology
  • syntax
  • semantics
  • discourse/pragmatics
  • (psycholinguistics)

Literature

  • Textbook for the morphology and syntax parts: E. Bender, 2013. "Linguistic Fundamentals for NLP" Can be accessed from within the UP network.
  • Additional online syntax textbook with excellent introduction to major concepts like constituency etc.: Santorini/Kroch The syntax of natural language
  • Textbook for semantics: B. Partee, A. ter Meulen, R.E. Wall, 1987. "Mathematical Methods in Linguistics".
  • Additional (introductory) textbook: Fromkin, Rodman, Hyams, "An Introduction to Language".
